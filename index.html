How it works

How AxonAI walks beside you

No clinic. No complicated equipment. Just your phone, a few minutes, and clear, gentle guidance — every step of the way.

Step 1

Record a few simple movements

Stand, walk a few steps, or raise an arm — at home, in your own time. Your phone camera is all you need.

Step 2

See your progress, clearly

AxonAI measures movement you can't see by eye and shows it as gentle, encouraging progress — week by week.

Step 3

Follow a plan made for you

Your exercises adapt as you improve, and your care team stays in the loop — so you're never doing this alone.

"The first time we saw the line going up, my dad cried. So did I."

Progress made visible is progress that keeps you going.

A plan made for you

A personalised rehab plan that adapts as you do.

Each day, RehynAI suggests a short, gentle set of exercises chosen for your stage of recovery. As your strength and balance improve, the plan quietly evolves with you — and your care team stays in the loop, so you're never doing this alone.

Meet AIRA

A rehab agent with a whole care team inside.

AIRA is RehynAI's rehabilitation agent, trained on the expertise of different therapists — physiotherapists, occupational therapists, cognitive therapists and more — so the guidance you get at home reflects how a real care team thinks.

Day to day, AIRA manages your chat, tracks your progress, keeps your hospital updated with how you're doing, and adjusts your rehab plan as you improve — quietly, in the background, so you can focus on getting better.
